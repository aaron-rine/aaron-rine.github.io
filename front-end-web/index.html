A Little Info on Eevees

Eevees are amazing creatures. They can evolve into a number of different pokemon, my favorite being Flareon. They are based on a number of real life animals such as:

  • foxes
  • cats
  • dogs
  • and rabbits

It is speculated that their coloring comes from the tanuki, a japanese racoon/dog hybrid.
